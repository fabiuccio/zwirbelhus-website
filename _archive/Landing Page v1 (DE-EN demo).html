Unseri Kita

A second home, full of light.

Die Zwirbelhus ist eine zentral gelegene, professionell und liebevoll geführte Kindertagesstätte. Helle, hohe Räume sind ein wesentliches Thema — thoughtfully planned spaces are an essential building block of valuable pedagogy.

Our warm-hearted, imaginative team keeps a respectful, transparent relationship with one another, with parents and with the children — so every child feels seen, understood and geborge.
